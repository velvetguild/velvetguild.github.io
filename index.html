More About Us



Myself (Dahid) and Reselda formed the guild in June 2017 after we decided we were tired of raiding 3 nights a week with players we barely spoke to. Many guild members would only interact with each other during raids; leaving an empty feeling in the guild. We created Velvet with the intentions of experiencing content, whilst enjoying the game with friends in a guild. Our philosophy is to play the game like it's meant to be played; for fun.

We welcome all members to our guild, whether you are a raider, pvper or someone who enjoys a bit of everything in WoW.
